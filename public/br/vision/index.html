br — Creative Vision & Publishing Plan (v0.1)

North star: finish meaningful work and put it in front of the right readers.
Themes: work & identity; leadership & clarity; family; American places; risk & reinvention.

Formats

Web essays · Books (non-fiction + fiction) · Screen (pilot/treatments) · Short-video readings · Audio.

Creative slate

  • Serendipity: A Tactical Life — memoir + business reflections. Ship 1 excerpt/month.
  • Clarity at Work — short handbook (20–30k). Compile essays + cases.
  • Elias Vance — thriller pilot/novel; 8–12p pilot draft.

Cadence & Workflow

Weekly: 1 web piece or 1k words. Monthly: 1 newsletter + 1 public excerpt. Quarterly: 1 story or 3-essay PDF.
Workflow: capture → vibe-code → draft (90m) → revise → publish (Ghost + newsletter) → archive.

Audience & Metrics

Direct list; LinkedIn; small podcasts; cross-links from RCM/R&C; local events.
Targets: 2–4k words/week, 3–4 posts/month, +50–100 subs/month, ~30% deep reads.
