RCM — Living Business Plan (v0.1a)

Updated: 2025-10-11

Promise: Ship a small, fast thing that works; teach the team to run it; improve weekly.
Guardrails: no heavy custom apps, enterprise e-comm, or 24/7 support.

Positioning

  • Category: Creative studio (brand/content/lightweight web) with practical AI.
  • ICP: Founder-led companies, boutique firms, solo experts, niche media.
  • Why us: Senior judgment, editorial taste, repeatable systems; ship-first bias.

Offers (repeatable)

  1. Lightweight Site Sprint (1–2 wks) — Fast pages, clean IA, basic SEO/meta, analytics, lead capture. Stack: Ghost+Summit / Notion / GA/Tag / Buttondown/Substack.
  2. Brand & Starter Content Kit (2–3 wks) — Voice + tokens + 5–7 pages copy + 3 posts + 6 social posts.
  3. Podcast/Video Starter (1–2 wks) — 1 publish-ready episode; script, Descript project, thumbnail template, upload flow, show notes.
  4. Cadence Retainer (monthly) — Weekly session; 2–4 shipped items/month; dashboard.

Pricing guidance: Site $3k–$8k · Brand Kit $5k–$12k · Pod/Video $2k–$6k · Retainer $1.5k–$4k/mo.

Process (golden path)

  1. Kickoff → goals, audience, constraints, definition of done.
  2. Vibe-coding → copy-first prototype; ship the smallest useful slice.
  3. Ship → live pages/posts/assets.
  4. Measure → speed, cadence, signups/leads.
  5. Iterate weekly → tiny deltas; no scope creep.

Service levels: async ≤ 24–48h weekdays; weekly 60–90m session.

Metrics we promise

  • Time-to-first-publish ≤ 7–10 days
  • Cadence 1–2 pieces/week after starter
  • Lighthouse "Good" on core pages
  • Lead capture live (CTA + tracking)

GTM & 90-Day Plan

Channels: br.com essays; LinkedIn; warm network; small podcasts; targeted intros.
Lead magnets: Founder Site Sprint (1-pager); Vibe-coding Runbook (90-min).

  • Month 1: Site slice + 4 posts; 1-pager; 3 intro calls.
  • Month 2: 1 client sprint; +4 posts; case note.
  • Month 3: 1–2 clients; +4 posts; launch cadence retainer.

Roles & Risks

Roles: Barry—strategy/editorial/taste; Griffin—prototypes/AI/integrations; Specialists—design (as needed). Capacity: 1 client now → 2 within 60–90 days.

Risks & mitigations: Scope creep → fixed scope + backlog; Design lift → specialist; Availability → holds + async templates.
