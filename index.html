Meet the Team

BR

Barry Rutherford

Founder & Creative Director

Former global CEO with 50 years of leadership experience across multiple continents and industries. Author, podcaster, and creative founder now based in Santa Fe. With over 8 million air miles logged, Barry brings a unique perspective that bridges corporate boardrooms and creative studios.

Current focus: A Tactical Life memoir, Interviews in the Past Tense fiction trilogy, and co-hosting the Malestrum podcast.

GR

Griffin Rutherford

Co-Host & Gen Z Voice

Recent engineering MS graduate and co-host of Malestrum. Griffin brings a fresh Gen Z perspective to cultural commentary and discussions about modern masculinity. An avid hiker and outdoorsman, he bridges the gap between academic rigor and authentic lived experience.

Current focus: Malestrum podcast, outdoor adventure content, and engineering projects that matter.
