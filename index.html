Breakwater's Approach

Strategy Before Tools

Most AI consultants start with technology. Breakwater starts with the business. What are the actual constraints? Where do people waste time? What decisions take too long?

Only after understanding operations does Breakwater consider if AI makes sense, and if it does, which specific applications might deliver measurable value.

No Hype, No Bullshit

AI is not magic. It's a tool. Sometimes it's the right tool. Often it's not. Barry Rutherford's fifty years of operating experience bring a clear filter for the difference between genuine capability and expensive complexity.

Consulting Only

Breakwater provides strategy, readiness review, audit, training, and advisory support. It does not sell bespoke AI builds, proprietary automation packages, or implementation shortcuts.

Core Principles

  • Assess first: Understand where you are before recommending where to go
  • Human-centered: AI augments people, doesn't replace judgment
  • Measurable wins: Clear ROI, not vague promises
  • Practical advisory: Recommendations stay grounded in what the organization can responsibly adopt
  • Change management: Technology without adoption is worthless
