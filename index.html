Rutherford Creative Media

Where Executive Experience Meets Authentic Storytelling

The umbrella brand for Barry Rutherford's creative, media, and consulting projects—bridging decades of global leadership with compelling narrative craft.

Creative Authority. Authentic Voice.

Rutherford Creative Media serves as both creative studio and publishing platform, where business wisdom meets storytelling craft.

After five decades as a global CEO, Barry Rutherford has turned his attention to the stories that shaped his journey and the narratives that define our time. RCM is the umbrella brand that houses his creative, media, and consulting ventures—each one informed by a lifetime of leadership experience and a commitment to authentic voice.

From the boardrooms of international corporations to the podcasting studios exploring modern masculinity, from memoir chapters that distill business wisdom to thriller fiction that examines the cost of secrets—RCM bridges worlds that rarely intersect with such intentionality.

This is where strategic thinking meets creative expression, where decades of real-world experience becomes the foundation for compelling storytelling.

Creative Works

Fiction

Elias Vance Series

"One man, haunted by old injuries and darker secrets, forced into action when corruption pushes too far."
Thriller/Action • In the tradition of Jack Reacher & Orphan X

Haunted by injuries that never healed and secrets that refuse to stay buried, Elias Vance lives quietly on society's edge. But when corruption threatens the people around him, the past he tried to escape drags him back into the fight. Part soldier, part survivor — Elias is a man who never asked to be a hero, but one who can't walk away when justice calls.

Memoir

A Tactical Life

Chapter: Serendipity

Looking back across five decades of business and life, I can see how often success wasn't the product of a perfect plan but of being ready when opportunity appeared. Serendipity isn't luck alone — it's a mix of preparation, timing, and the willingness to act when doors unexpectedly open. From chance encounters on factory floors to meetings in far-flung cities, those unplanned moments shaped the trajectory of my career more than any spreadsheet or forecast ever did.
