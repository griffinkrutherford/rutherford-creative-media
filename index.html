From the boardrooms of international corporations to the podcasting studios exploring modern masculinity, from memoir chapters that distill business wisdom to thriller fiction that examines the cost of secrets—RCM bridges worlds that rarely intersect with such intentionality.

This is where strategic thinking meets creative expression, where decades of real-world experience becomes the foundation for compelling storytelling.

What We Implement

Engagements are modular. Start where the pain is worst, expand after first wins.

AI Triage & Fix

  • Stabilize flaky automations, prompts, and brittle integrations.
  • Diagnose failure points across tools (ChatGPT/Claude, Descript, Notion, Ghost/Substack, Make/Zapier, GHL, etc.).
  • Harden: retries, fallbacks, logging, permissions, cost controls.
Outcome: Your current stack stops breaking in production hours.

Workflow Linking

  • Map your content/data flows end-to-end; remove swivel-chair steps.
  • Unify prompts, templates, and data sources; reduce duplicate work.
  • API-level stitching for reliability; human checkpoints where needed.
Outcome: A coherent system where inputs, outputs, and ownership are clear.

AI Collaboration Pods

  • Stand up small cross-functional pods with embedded AI assistants.
  • Define operating cadences, review rituals, and prompt/tool libraries.
  • Create role-specific 'copilots' for PMs, operators, creators, execs.
Outcome: Teams that actually co-work with AI instead of fighting it.

Vibe-Coding Workshops

  • Teach non-developers rapid AI-assisted building (Claude/ChatGPT).
  • Patterns: spec → prompt → code → test → refine, in tight loops.
  • Ship micro-tools: content pipelines, dashboards, CRUD utilities.
Outcome: Staff who can self-serve 80% of needs without a dev queue.

Advisory & Retainers

  • Executive guidance on AI roadmaps, staffing, partner/tool choices.
  • Quarterly architecture reviews, prompt ops governance, KPIs.
  • Board-level updates translating AI work into business outcomes.
Outcome: Clarity at the top; momentum in the middle; results at the edge.

Compliance & Guardrails

  • Lightweight governance for privacy, IP, and data security.
  • Human-in-the-loop checkpoints; red-team prompts; audit trails.
  • Model/tool selection aligned with policy and risk posture.
Outcome: Speed with sensible safety — no innovation freeze.
